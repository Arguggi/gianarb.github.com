There is a new YouTube live show in town!

The idea is to host open source maintainers, contributors that are happy to share with me one hour of their life coding on a project they like. We are gonna see what they do, and who their setup looks like!

  • Are you an open source maintainer? let's review, merge and release something together.
  • Are you a contributor? Let's try some documentation or let's open a PR!
  • Do you like the idea to code together for an hour?! Let's join!

I will be your noisy neighbour! Reach out to ciao[at]gianarb[dot]it or via twitter @gianarb my DMs are open.

Subscribe to the YouTube channel to receive a notification when a live starts. I keep a newsletter if you like the idea!
