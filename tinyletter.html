What is this email about?

With this subscription, you will get a be-weekly(sh) letter in your inbox full of links, contents, events about Go, SRE, Cloud Computing, Open source, and programming that I created, discovered, read that inspired me in a way or another.

As you know, I like to build and to share what I do, but there are a lot of different platforms I use: Twitter, other blog posts, youtube videos, podcasts.

A newsletter is a way to stay in touch, no matter the platform you follow.

Why am I subscribed?

Across many years of activity as creator, I build a list of emails that will come from my blog post or scaledocker.com; for months, I never sent any email because I didn't have an editor line. So if you do not recall why you get my email, I am sorry, there is always a link to unsubscribe, and I promise you won't hear from me any longer.
